Conclusion

The science discovered that there is no relationship between religion and the number of children in the family.
To decrease the number of children in the family we have to:
  • Decrease Poverty: in poor families children are cheap labor force. Poor families need a lot of children to survive;
  • Decrease Children Mortality: if children mortality is high then families want to have a lot of children. Then at least few of them will survive;
  • Enable family planing: contraception enables family planing;
  • Enable education of women: educated women don't want to have a lot of children. They want only few with good life;
  • Enable women to work: working women don't want to have a lot of children. They want only few with good life.
The science assumes that the world population will be 10 billion people. On this level will be stable - it will not increase or decrease.
