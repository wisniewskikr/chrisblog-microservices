Ninh Binh Town

The town Ninh Binh is the capitol of Ninh Binh district. Very well communicated with Hanoi and with large amount of hotels and restaurants it's a good base for visitors to explore the surrounding area.

Trang An

Trang An is around 7 kilometers from Ninh Binh town. It's known for its scenic water ways and you can hire a boat here and float along the Sao Khe River. You can choose here one of three posible routes. The price is the same.

Tam Coc

Tam Coc is known for its karst landscape which is similar to the better known Halong Bay, although here you will find some scenic limestone hills which are dotted amongst emerald green rice paddies.

The Bich Dong Pagoda is located on the side of a mountain and is made up of three levels called the Ha, Trung and Thuong Pagodas.

Cuc Phuong National Park

Cuc Phuong National Park is one of the main national parks in Vietnam and was established in 1962. This is part of a huge swathe of protected land in Ninh Binh and was set up in order to take care of the wildlife in the region which has been in decline for many years as a result of poaching.

Van Long Nature Reserve

Van Long Nature Reserve is a protected area of land, it is teeming with local flora and fauna, so if you are a nature and want to check out the Vietnamese wildlife then don`t miss it off the itinerary.

Dich Long Pagoda includes also Dich Long Cave. It's legendary that the cave was found out in 1739 with many stalitiles and stalimites especially buddha-like one and it is worshipped since.
