Ha Giang Town

The province of Ha Giang is located in the far north of Vietnam, surrounded by Cao Bang, Tuyen Quang, Lao Cai and Yen Bai, and contains Vietnam's northernmost point. Ha Giang town, where most tourists will start exploring the province, is located 320 km from Hanoi. The most famous attraction in Ha Giang province is a four day motorbike tour.

Day 1 of Motorbike Tour: Ha Giang - Yen Ming. Lung Khuy Cave.

Lung Khuy Cave is one of the most beautiful caves in the rocky plateau, is a masterpiece created by millennia of geological activities. This cave is around 300m in length with many different branches, magnificent stalactites and fancy shapes stimulating the imagination of travellers.

Day 2 of Motorbike Tour: Yen Ming - Dong Van. Lung Cu Flag Tower.

Lung Cu Flag Tower is a monument in northern Vietnam. The monument consists of a 30-metre tall tower on the summit of Lung Cu Peak (over 1,400 metres above sea level). The tower is topped with a large Vietnamese flag. The monument was built to mark the northernmost point in Vietnam, although the farthest north point on the Chinese-Vietnamese border is actually located over 3.3 km farther north.
