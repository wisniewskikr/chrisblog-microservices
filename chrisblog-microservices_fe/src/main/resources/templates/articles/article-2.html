Why people are ignorant about the world?

People are ignorant about the world because they have skewed information. And they have skewed information because:
  • Personal Bias: mostly people have contact only with other people who are similar to them. Very rarely they have contact with people from other cultures with differents points of views.
  • Outdated Facts: teachers teach facts which they have learned in the past. Very often these facts are outdated.
  • News Bias: news presents only events which are interesting for audience. They dosen't present facts which are boring but take place more often. For instance death by car accident vs by stroke.

How to not be ignorant about the world?

In order to not be ignorant about the world you have to follow these four rules:
  • Most Things Improve: statistic shows that most things in the world improves. Every day is better and better.
  • One Hump: statistic shows that there is no division for reach and poor countries any more. Now most of the countries is in the middle.
  • First Social, then Rich: statistic shows that countries improve social first. And then they become rich.
  • Sharks Kill Few: if you are afraid of something then probably you exaggerate danger. In reality risk is not so high.
